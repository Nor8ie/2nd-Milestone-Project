Rock Paper Scissors

  1. Press "Click to Play!" button to choose a username;
  2. Press Enter to start the game. Alternatively press "Cancel" to continue with default "Player" name;
  3. Left handside of the screen contains your choice buttons (Paper, Scissors, Rock) as well as your hand and scoreboard;
  4. Once you choose a hand, Computer will do the same;
  5. Hands get compared to decide the winner:
    • A player who decides to play rock will beat another player who has chosen scissors ("rock crushes scissors"), but
    • Will lose to one who has played paper ("paper covers rock")
    • A play of paper will lose to a play of scissors ("scissors cuts paper").
    • If both players choose the same shape, the game is tied and is usually immediately replayed to break the tie
  6. The winner side and scoreboard will light up to mark the victory
  7. The Round Counter (top left side of the screen) will show the number of the rounds played, maximum turns is 10.
  8. Game will reset after round 10 or anytime when "Click to Play" button is pressed.
